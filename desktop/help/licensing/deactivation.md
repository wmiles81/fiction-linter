## Deactivation

If you are moving Fiction Linter to a new machine, deactivate your license on the old one first. This releases the activation slot so the key can be used elsewhere.

### How to deactivate

1. Open the **Help** menu in the menu bar at the top of the screen.
2. Click **Deactivate License...**.
3. Confirm the action when prompted.

Fiction Linter removes the stored license file from your machine and contacts LemonSqueezy to release the activation. After deactivation, the app returns to the activation screen on next launch.

### Then activate on the new machine

Install Fiction Linter on your new computer and enter your license key at the activation screen. As long as your key has an available activation slot, it will validate normally.

### If you cannot deactivate first

If your old machine is lost, damaged, or no longer accessible:

1. Log in to your LemonSqueezy customer account.
2. Find your Fiction Linter license and manually release activations from the dashboard.
3. Then activate on your new machine as normal.

If you have trouble, contact support at [ocotilloquillpress.com](https://ocotilloquillpress.com) with your purchase email address.
