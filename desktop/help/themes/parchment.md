## Parchment

**Parchment** is the theme Fiction Linter opens with the first time you launch the app. It evokes a warm afternoon writing desk: cream paper, soft shadows, and terracotta accent tones drawn from aged manuscript pages.

### Design intent

Parchment is optimized for long daytime writing sessions in natural or warm artificial light. The slightly off-white background reduces the harshness of a pure-white screen while keeping contrast high enough for comfortable extended reading.

### Colors at a glance

- **Editor background** — warm cream (off-white with a slight yellow cast)
- **Body text** — near-black with a warm brown undertone
- **Accents and active elements** — terracotta / burnt orange
- **Toolbar and UI chrome** — light tan with subtle borders

### Findings underlines on Parchment

The red, orange, and blue severity underlines are clearly visible against the cream background. The warm palette makes orange warnings blend slightly more with the background than on cooler themes — if you find orange hard to spot, try Sepia or High Contrast instead.

### Switching away

To change themes, click the **theme picker dropdown** in the **top-right corner** of the window.
